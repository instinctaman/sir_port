About Mr. Sujeet Roy

Mr. Sujeet Roy is a dynamic entrepreneur, philanthropist, and educationist with a visionary approach to business and social responsibility. He has pioneered multiple ventures across diverse industries, including education, hospitality, technology, and social impact. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, he continues to lead with innovation and integrity.

His leadership is defined by a relentless pursuit of empowering individuals and communities. Through his strategic initiatives, he fosters sustainable growth, bridges the gap between industry and academia, and drives social change. His contributions, particularly through Nirvan Trust, have positively impacted countless lives, ensuring opportunities for those in need.
